8 Days - 7 Nights (Selous, Mikumi, Ruaha)

8 Days - 7 Nights (Selous, Mikumi, Ruaha)

Explore Tour

Descriptions

Welcome to Mikumi National parks rich in wildlife like buffalo, elephant, lions, wildebeests, Maasai giraffe, hippos, impalas, and crocodile, there are also, zebras and wart hogs. Most of the animals can be seen at all seasons, flying from Zanzibar to Mikumi National park is about 2:45 hours, this will be a camping safari with tents or lodge, during the night you will hear lots of animals, including hyena, sometimes even a lion, also itinerary can be customized depending on your needs and budget

Tour Plan

  • Arrive in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania's largest city, and transfer to your hotel.

  • Depending on your arrival time, you may have the opportunity to explore the city's markets or relax after your journey.

  • After breakfast, depart for Selous Game Reserve, one of the largest reserves in Africa.

  • Enjoy a game drive en route to your lodge, where you'll have lunch upon arrival.

  • In the afternoon, embark on a thrilling boat safari along the Rufiji River, spotting hippos, crocodiles, and a variety of bird species.

  • Return to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

  • Spend the day exploring Selous Game Reserve on morning and afternoon game drives, led by experienced guides.

  • Selous is known for its diverse wildlife, including elephants, lions, leopards, wild dogs, and abundant birdlife.

  • Take a break during the heat of the day to relax at the lodge or enjoy a guided nature walk.

  • Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

  • Depart Selous after breakfast and drive to Mikumi National Park, known for its open grasslands and proximity to the Selous Game Reserve.

  • Arrive at your lodge in time for lunch, followed by a leisurely afternoon game drive in Mikumi, where you may encounter giraffes, zebras, buffaloes, and more.

  • Return to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

  • Spend the day exploring Mikumi National Park on game drives, searching for wildlife against the backdrop of the Uluguru Mountains.

  • Mikumi is home to a variety of animals, including lions, elephants, hippos, and a wide array of bird species.

  • Enjoy picnic lunches in the park and continue your wildlife encounters until late afternoon.

  • Return to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

  • After breakfast, depart for Ruaha National Park, known for its rugged terrain, baobab-studded landscapes, and large concentrations of wildlife.

  • Arrive at your lodge in time for lunch, followed by an afternoon game drive in Ruaha, where you may spot lions, elephants, giraffes, and more.

  • Return to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

  • Spend the day exploring Ruaha National Park on morning and afternoon game drives, with opportunities to encounter predators and prey in this remote and wild landscape.

  • Ruaha is renowned for its large elephant herds, as well as its populations of lions, leopards, cheetahs, and rare species such as the African wild dog.

  • Enjoy a bush breakfast or picnic lunch during your game drives. Return to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

  • After breakfast, depart Ruaha National Park and drive back to Dar es Salaam.

  • Depending on your flight schedule, you may have some free time in Dar es Salaam for last-minute shopping or sightseeing
