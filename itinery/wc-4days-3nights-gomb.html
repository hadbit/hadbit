4 Days - 3 Nights Gombe

4 Days - 3 Nights Gombe

Explore Tour

Descriptions

Welcome to Mikumi National parks rich in wildlife like buffalo, elephant, lions, wildebeests, Maasai giraffe, hippos, impalas, and crocodile, there are also, zebras and wart hogs. Most of the animals can be seen at all seasons, flying from Zanzibar to Mikumi National park is about 2:45 hours, this will be a camping safari with tents or lodge, during the night you will hear lots of animals, including hyena, sometimes even a lion, also itinerary can be customized depending on your needs and budget

Tour Plan

  • Arrive at the nearest airport or designated meeting point for Gombe National Park.

  • Transfer to your accommodation within or nearby the park.

  • After settling in, enjoy a welcome briefing and orientation about the park and its conservation efforts.

  • Take a leisurely walk around the lodge area, soaking in the serene surroundings and possibly spotting some wildlife.

  • Dinner and overnight stay at the lodge.

  • Early morning breakfast before embarking on a guided chimpanzee trekking adventure.

  • Accompanied by experienced guides, venture into the lush forest in search of chimpanzee families.

  • Spend the morning observing these fascinating primates in their natural habitat, learning about their behaviors and social dynamics.

  • Enjoy a packed lunch amidst the forest, surrounded by the sights and sounds of nature.

  • Return to the lodge in the afternoon for relaxation and leisure time.

  • Dinner and overnight stay at the lodge.

  • After breakfast, set out for a guided forest walk to explore the diverse flora and fauna of Gombe National Park.

  • Learn about the various plant species, birdlife, and smaller mammals that call the forest home.

  • Return to the lodge for lunch and a brief rest.

  • In the afternoon, embark on a scenic boat safari on Lake Tanganyika, the second deepest lake in the world.

  • Cruise along the shoreline, marveling at the stunning landscapes and observing wildlife such as hippos, crocodiles, and diverse bird species.

  • Return to the lodge in the evening for dinner and relaxation.

  • Enjoy a final breakfast at the lodge, soaking in the tranquil atmosphere of Gombe National Park.

  • Depending on departure times, there may be an opportunity for one last activity, such as a short nature walk or birdwatching session.

  • Check out from the lodge and transfer to the airport or designated departure point, bidding farewell to Gombe with cherished memories of your wildlife encounters and jungle adventures.
